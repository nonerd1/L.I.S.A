Performance Comparison

Below is a comparison of key performance metrics between normal configuration (arms retracted) and extended configuration.

MetricNormalExtendedDifferenceNotes
Flight Time18 min15 min-16.7%Extension mechanism and increased drag reduce flight time.
Top Speed35 km/h30 km/h-14.3%Extended arms create more drag, reducing top speed.
Stability (Wind)MediumHigh+40%Extended arms significantly improve stability in windy conditions.
ManeuverabilityHighMedium-25%Extended arms reduce agility but improve precision hovering.
Power Consumption110W130W+18.2%Extension increases power draw due to higher air resistance.

PID Tuning Results
